Hotel Information

With shopping and travel hubs close-by, ITC Grand Hilton Hotel - Member ITC Hotels Group is well-connected for the travelers. This beach-side, village-style resort's architecture draws inspiration from the Indo-Portuguese vintage and blends classical elegance with a distinctive regional allure that's infused in its service, cuisine, rituals and more - promising a truly immersive experience

Placed at a mere distance from Arossim Beach, ITC Grand Hilton Hotel - Member ITC Hotels Group provides comfortable accommodation in Goa featuring swimming pool over multiple levels, with a breath-taking view of the Arabian Sea.

Hotel Facilities

Some facilities include 24-hour front desk, swimming pool, gymnasium, banqueting facility and kids play area to name a few. One can rejuvenate at Kaya Kalp - The Royal Spa. ITC Grand Goa Resort Spa - Member ITC Hotels Group in Goa also offers services like 24-hour room service and concierge to its guests.

Room Facilities

It comprises of 252 well-kept and spacious rooms spread across 4 floors. Each room has amenities like LCD television, temperature controller, in-room safe, packaged drinking water and bathroom with shower facility. It also features personal outdoor spaces in the form of a balcony or patio. Few of the rooms have outdoor rain showers secret gardens.

Dining

The property also has 6 signature food and beverage outlets. Pavilion at the Village Square serving Indian and International Cuisine. Ottimo Cucina Italiana serving Italian cuisine. Tempero which serves Goan cuisine, Kebabs Kurries offers heritage Indian cuisine. Enjoy seaside barbeque grill at The Royal Afghan followed with the Signature Goan restaurant.

Important Landmarks

The ITC Grand Goa Resort Spa - Member ITC Hotels Group in Goa is accessible via travel hubs such as Cansaulim Railway Station (3 km) and Goa International Airport (16 km). Places worth a visit include Velsao Beach (5 km) and Colva Beach (8 km).

What people love the most about this property is its great location and classy ambience. Book now and enjoy world class services at ITC Grand Goa Resort Spa - Member ITC Hotels Group. Questions & Answers

How do I know my reservation was booked ?

You will receive an SMS and email on confirmation of your hotel(s) booking.

Do I need to confirm my reservation ?

There is no need to confirm your reservation. If you still feel you would like to verify that your reservation was made, you can do so by writing to our Customer Support Team or by contacting our customer services team.

How do I cancel/amend a hotel(s) reservation?

To cancel a booking log in to My Bookings section or call our call center on 0124-4180820 (all networks)
To amend a booking call our call center on 0124-4180820 (all networks).

Is there a cancellation policy for hotel(s) booked on Yatra.com?

The cancellation policy for hotel(s) bookings varies according to hotel(s) and room type. For more information, please check the cancellation policy mentioned next to the price for the room type. If you are cancelling after the check-in date, there will be no refund. In all cases, you'll be charged a standard cancellation fee of Rs. 250 per booking over and above the hotel(s)'s own cancellation charges.

How do I pay for the gala dinner?

Gala dinner charges which are applicable for Christmas and New Year dates would be extra and payable directly to the hotel(s). Please check with the hotel(s) directly for more information on the same.
